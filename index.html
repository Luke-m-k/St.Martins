St Martins De Porres Catholic church

Saint Martin de Porres Catholic Church - Mugutha, was founded on the first Holy Mass, 2nd November 2003 as the result of a consolidation of the two Small Christian Communities, namely St. Charles Lwanga and St. Monica which were affiliated from St. Christopher Catholic Church - Kimbo an Outstation of the St. Francis of Assisi Catholic Parish, Ruiru.

The first Holy Mass on 2nd November 2003 was hosted at the residence of Mr.& Mrs. Vincent Kariuki Kiwara, who volunteered to host the Church until it was able to acquire its home. We thank God that our Outstation has grown both in numbers and development. We currently have approximately 200 families divided into sixteen (16) Small Christian Communities: St. Lawrence, St. Claudia, St. Marcellina, St. Isidore the Farmer, St. Matthew, St. John Paul II, St. Catherine of Egypt, St. Josephine Bhakhita, St. Veronica Guiliani, St. Benard, St.Anthony of Padua, St. Titus, St. Scholastica, and St. Gregory.

Two Small Christian communities were established in 2022. On 11th Dec 2022, we celebrated our outstation family that was graced by His Lordship Bishop George Muthaka, bishop of Garissa Diocese. Fifteen(15) couples renewed their marriage vows and three(3) couples received the sacra-ment of Holy Matrimony. In April 2023, eighteen(18) infants were baptised and six(6) catechumens were baptized in August. Twenty-two(22) candidates received the Sacrament of First Holy Communion while Twenty(20) candidates were confirmed. Holy Mass on Sundays is at 8:00 a.m. We are grateful to our priests for enabling Holy Mass Celebrations on other days. CWA celebrate Holy Mass every last Tuesday of the Month in the church and prayers every Tuesday in the church or in a member's home, CMA celebrate Holy Mass every second Friday of the Month on a rotational basis at the home of one of the members.

Our church, by the grace of God, is at the last stages of construction. We are currently holding a pew drive which is halfway through as well as constructing a perimeter wall that is in its last stages. Members of the Local Council Executive are Mr Bernard Mwangi(Moderator), Mr George Ruhara(Vice Moderator), Mrs. Susan Njoroge(Secretary), Mr Joseph Gatheru(Vice Secretary) and Ms. Margaret Gachinga(Treasurer).

Mass

Our church hosts one Sunday Mass that begins at 8:00 am. Mass is animated by either one church group or one Small Christian Community. Special Mass, such as holiday Mass, are typically announced in advance.

Confessions

Confessions are available when announced by Our Parish St. Francis. Note that special requests can be made for Confessions after any Mass.

Baptism

Baptism is available upon official announcement from the Parish.

Catechism

Catechism classes for children are held at 2pm on Saturdays in the church, while adult catechism classes are available at the St. Francis Parish.

Weddings

Weddings are booked through the St. Francis Parish office after which marriage preparation classes are offered by our catechist.
